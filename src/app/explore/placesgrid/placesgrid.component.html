LalBagh Botanical Garden

A haven for all nature lovers, Lal Bagh is nationally and internationally renowned centre for botanical artwork. The garden features rare plants of French, Persian and Afghani origin. This botanical garden, also consists of the famous glass house where an annual flower show is held every year.

National Gallery Of Modern Art

This art gallery houses over 14,000 incredible works of contemporary and modern artists, the top notch collection include spectacular paintings and sculptures from Raja Ravi Verma, Jamini Roy, Amrita Sher-Gil, and Rabindranath Tagore. The depository showcases cultural ethos of India from periods as early as the 18th century.

Bangalore Palace

The Bangalore Palace was built to resemble Windsor Castle. This quirky palace is filled with an interesting and eclectic collection of art and paraphernalia from a bygone era. The palace grounds are a popular venue for rock concerts and other music events.

Indira Gandhi Musical Fountain Park

This Musical Fountain is probably one of the biggest artificially created fountains in India. The magnanimous fountains surrounded by lush gardens hosts two nightly light and sound shows per day. The show together with rhythmic dancing of the sparkling water is an enchanting site.

Bull Temple

The Bull Temple, also known as Nandi Temple, is one of the oldest temples in the city of Bengaluru and and is the biggest temple dedicated to Nandi in the world. The entire sculpture of the bull is carved out of one single granite rock. The statue is 4.5 meters high and 6.5 meters long.

6:00 AM - 8:00 PM ~5 km from Chancery

ISKCON Temple

ISKCON temple is a cultural complex accommodating dedicated deities of Sri Sri Radha Krishnachandra, Sri Sri Krishna Balarama, Sri Sri Nitai Gauranga, Sri Srinivasa Govinda and Sri Prahlada Narasimha. The temple community conducts a wide variety of activities directed towards rediscovering spirituality on an individual level as well as creating a spirit of harmony in the community.

7:15AM - 8:30 PM ~9.8 km from Chancery

Ulsoor Lake

One of the largest lakes in Bangalore, sit back or walk in the periphery of the park or click a few shots of the endemic birds that call this destination their home. One of the most popular activities in Ulsoor Lake is boating. Closed on Wednesdays.
